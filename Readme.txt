Game overview 

  GOAL
  The action takes place in a labyrinth. The main character has to find a given 
  number of keys to open the gates to exit the current and rise to the next 
  level.The keys lie hidden in the labyrinth walls. To get at the keys the hero 
  has to blast the walls using mines and bombs. The hero has an unlimited number 
  of bombs and 20 mines at the most. By gathering treasures scattered all over 
  the labyrinth the hero scores points. The hero's mission is under threat from 
  the monsters who impare him at collisions or by shooting. The hero can kill 
  the monsters with bombs and mines. By doing so he scores additional points.


  HERO PARAMETERS
  You can play for one of the two heros. BOMBERIC is a gallant adventurous 
  fox-cub. CARLOS is a brave noble knight. 

  The hero's parameters include:
  - Life
  - Hit-points. (HP).
  One life is equal to 100 HP 

  When damaged the hero loses a certain number of hit-points. Losing 100 
  hit-points means losing a life. At the beginning the hero is given 5 lives. He 
  can replace the hit-points by gathering special bonuses (HEALTH-BONUSES). The 
  hero can also return lives by collecting a special bonus (LIVE) or scoring 
  5000 points per life. 


  BONUSES
  There are several types of bonuses: 

  BONUS-GOLD
  By collecting these bonuses the hero scores points:
  - RUBY (100)
  - EMERALD (120)
  - COIN (50)
  - COINS (150)
  - DRAHMA (300)
  - TOPAZ (400)

  BONUS-HEALTH
  By collecting these bonuses the hero scores HPs or lives. 

  HP bonuses include:
  - SMALL HEALTH (20)
  - MEDIUM HEALTH (50)
  - BIG HEALTH (100)

  Life bonuse
  - LIVE (1)


  ACTION-BONUSES

  These bonuses evoke specific actions and are effective for a fixed period of 
  time. These bonuses can produce positive and negative effects.

  The following bonuses are positive
  - SHIELD (the hero becomes impregnable)
  - CRUSH (the hero becomes impregnable and on colliding with him the monster 
  blows to pieces)
  - EYE (the hero is enabled to see invisible things)
  - STOP (all the enemies are haulted)

  The following bonuses are negative
  - SHACKLES (the hero is slowed down)
  - BOMBFREEZE (the hero is disabled to install bombs)



  MONSTERS
  FLOATER . A semi-intelligent creature more like a large mercury ball in 
  appearance randomly moving all over the labyrinth at high speed.

  IRON HEAD . The labyrinth's protector. He moves along the labyrinth and as 
  soon as he notices the hero he speeds up in his direction. 

  DARK KNIGTH. Monster-hunter. He can shoot at the hero and is very dangerous at 
  collision. 

  JUMPER. Monster-kamikaze. This is a hopping bomb that moves haphazardly at 
  high speed and blows up on collision with the hero. 

  REX. Rex is the most dangerous monster. He is extremely powerful, capable of 
  shooting explosive shells. He consistutes the greatest menace for the hero. 
  You will meet this monster every ten levels of the labyrinth. For killing a 
  REX you get 10000 points at once!
